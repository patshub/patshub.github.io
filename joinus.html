JOIN OUR FAMILY

VOLUNTEERING

There are several ways you can get involved with FCS from becoming a General Volunteer to Board Member. Find out which one fits you best!

GENERAL VOLUNTEERS

Become a general volunteer where you can tailor volunteering to your personal schedule throughout the year.

SUMMER VOLUNTEERS

Are you ready to teach the next generation about Filipino culture? Want to help out in the classrooms? Become a summer volunteer.

COMMITTEE MEMBERS

Do you have a special skill that can help further our mission of cultural education and make an impact on the community? Join us today!

FCS BLOGGER

Do you have opinions and knowledge that the current and next generation of filipino americans should know about? Want to educate the community through discussion? Become an FCS blogger!

INTERESTED? GET IN TOUCH!

Whether you want to help us outreach throughout the year, volunteer in the summer, or share your knowledge about the culture, contact us so we can have you on board!
